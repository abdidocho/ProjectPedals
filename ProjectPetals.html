Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right size bike?

Use your height and inseam as a guide and try test rides whenever possible.

What accessories should I have?

Helmet, lock, lights, water bottle, patch kit, and padded shorts are essentials.

How do I fix a flat tire?

Remove tire, patch or replace the tube, inflate, and reattach.

How do I adjust bike brakes?

Align pads, adjust tension, and ensure they stop evenly.

How do I choose tire pressure?

Check sidewall markings and use a pump with a pressure gauge for best results.
