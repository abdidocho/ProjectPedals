Our Services

Bike Repairs

Starting at $10

Our Basic Repairs service is perfect for regular maintenance and quick fixes to keep your bike safe, smooth, and road-ready. Whether you're dealing with squeaky brakes, a skipping chain, or a soft tire, we've got you covered. These small but essential repairs help prevent bigger problems down the road and ensure every ride is a good one.

Basic Tune-Ups

Starting at $50

Our Beginner Tune-Up is designed for casual riders, new bike owners, or anyone looking to get their bike checked out without diving into a full overhaul. It’s a great way to make sure your bike is safe, smooth, and ready to ride — whether it's fresh out of the garage or fresh off the rack.

Standard/Intermediate Tune-Up

Starting at $80

Our Intermediate Tune-Up is ideal for riders who hit the road or trail more often and want their bike running at peak performance. It goes beyond the basics, focusing on fine-tuning key components, ensuring reliability, and preventing long-term wear.

Comprehensive Tune-Ups

Starting at $150

Our Comprehensive Tune-Up is built for serious cyclists, high-mileage commuters, or bikes that haven’t seen a shop in a while. This deep-dive service includes everything from precision adjustments to a full drivetrain cleaning and component inspection. It’s like a spa day for your bike — thorough, restorative, and worth every turn of the wrench.
